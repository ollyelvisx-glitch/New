28 Fundamental Beliefs

1. The Doctrine of God
1. The Holy Scriptures

The Bible is the inspired, infallible word of God—the ultimate rule of faith.

2 Timothy 3:16
2. The Trinity

There is one God: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—a unity of three co-eternal Persons.

Matthew 28:19
3. The Father

God the Eternal Father is the Creator, Source, Sustainer, and Sovereign of all creation.

John 3:16
4. The Son

God the Eternal Son became incarnate in Jesus Christ. Through Him, all things were created.

John 1:1-3
5. The Holy Spirit

God the Eternal Spirit was active with the Father and the Son in Creation and Incarnation.

John 16:7-13
2. The Doctrine of Humanity
6. Creation

God is Creator of all things, and has revealed in Scripture the authentic account of His creative activity in a literal six-day week.

Genesis 1-2
7. Nature of Humanity

Man and woman were made in the image of God with individuality, power, and freedom to think and do.

Genesis 1:26-28
3. The Doctrine of Salvation
8. The Great Controversy

All humanity is now involved in a cosmic conflict between Christ and Satan regarding the character of God.

Revelation 12:4-9
9. Life, Death, and Resurrection of Christ

In Christ's life of perfect obedience, His suffering, death, and resurrection, God provided the only means of atonement.

Romans 5:8
10. Experience of Salvation

In infinite love and mercy God made Christ, who knew no sin, to be sin for us, so that we might be justified.

Ephesians 2:4-10
11. Growing in Christ

By His death on the cross Jesus triumphed over the forces of evil. We are empowered to grow spiritually.

Colossians 1:13-14
4. The Doctrine of the Church
12. The Church

The church is the community of believers who confess Jesus Christ as Lord and Saviour.

Ephesians 1:22-23
13. The Remnant and Its Mission

In the last days, a remnant is called out to keep the commandments of God and the faith of Jesus.

Revelation 14:6-12
14. Unity in the Body of Christ

The church is one body with many members, called from every nation, kindred, tongue, and people.

Galatians 3:27-29
15. Baptism

By baptism we confess our faith in the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ. Done by full immersion.

Romans 6:1-6
16. The Lord’s Supper

Participation in the emblems of the body and blood of Jesus. Prefaced by the ordinance of foot-washing.

John 13:1-17
17. Spiritual Gifts and Ministries

God bestows upon all members of His church in every age spiritual gifts to employ in loving ministry.

Romans 12:4-8
18. The Gift of Prophecy

One of the identifying gifts of the remnant church is the gift of prophecy, manifested in the ministry of Ellen G. White.

Revelation 19:10
5. The Doctrine of the Christian Life
19. Law of God

The great principles of God's law are embodied in the Ten Commandments and exemplified in the life of Christ.

Exodus 20:1-17
20. The Sabbath

The beneficent Creator, after the six days of Creation, rested on the seventh day and instituted the Sabbath.

Luke 4:16
21. Stewardship

We are God's stewards, entrusted with time, opportunities, and possessions. We return tithes and offerings.

Malachi 3:8-12
22. Christian Behavior

We are called to be a godly people who think, feel, and act in harmony with biblical principles.

1 Corinthians 10:31
23. Marriage and the Family

Marriage was divinely instituted in Eden and affirmed by Jesus to be a lifelong union between a man and a woman.

Matthew 19:3-9
6. The Doctrine of Last Things
24. Christ’s Ministry in the Heavenly Sanctuary

There is a sanctuary in heaven. In it, Christ ministers on our behalf, making available His atonement.

Hebrews 8:1-5
25. Second Coming of Christ

The second coming of Christ is the blessed hope of the church. It will be literal, visible, and worldwide.

Revelation 1:7
26. Death and Resurrection

Until the resurrection, death is an unconscious sleep state for all people.

Ecclesiastes 9:5-6
27. Millennium and the End of Sin

The millennium is the thousand-year reign of Christ with His saints in heaven between the resurrections.

Revelation 20
28. New Earth

On the new earth, God will provide an eternal home for the redeemed and a perfect environment for everlasting life.

Revelation 21:1-7
